# Games Guide

## Casino Games

### Dice Game
**How to Play:**
- Choose your target multiplier (1-6x)
- Place your bet
- Roll the dice
- Win if the result meets your target

**Odds:**
- 1x: 83.3% (1.2x payout)
- 2x: 66.7% (1.5x payout)
- 3x: 50% (2x payout)
- 4x: 33.3% (3x payout)
- 5x: 16.7% (6x payout)
- 6x: 1.7% (60x payout)

**House Edge:** 5%

### Crash Game
**How to Play:**
- Set your bet amount
- Watch the multiplier rise
- Click "Cashout" to collect winnings before crash
- If you don't cashout before crash, you lose

**Strategy:**
- Set auto-cashout for consistent wins
- Higher multipliers have lower probability
- Manage risk vs reward

**House Edge:** 3%

### Mines Game
**How to Play:**
- Choose number of mines (1-24)
- Reveal tiles to avoid mines
- Each safe tile reveals multiplies your payout
- Hit a mine = game over

**Multiplier per Safe Tile:**
- 1 mine: ~1.05x per tile
- 5 mines: ~1.1x per tile
- 10 mines: ~1.2x per tile
- 24 mines: ~2.5x per tile

**House Edge:** 5%

### Color Prediction
**How to Play:**
- Predict the color: Red, Black, or Green
- Odds are similar to roulette

**Odds:**
- Red or Black: 1.95x
- Green: 17.5x

**House Edge:** 5%

### Roulette
**How to Play:**
- Place bets on numbers or colors
- Watch the wheel spin
- Win if your prediction matches

**Bet Types:**
- Color (Red/Black): 1.95x
- Even/Odd: 1.95x
- Number: 36x

**House Edge:** 2.7%

### Plinko
**How to Play:**
- Drop ball through pegs
- Ball falls into slots at bottom
- Each slot has different multiplier

**Multipliers:**
- 1.5x, 2x, 2.5x, 3x, 3.5x, 3x, 2.5x, 2x, 1.5x

**House Edge:** 5%

### Blackjack
**How to Play:**
- Get closer to 21 than dealer without going over
- Hit: Draw another card
- Stand: Keep your total
- Double: Double bet and get one card
- Split: Split matching cards

**Payouts:**
- Blackjack (21 with 2 cards): 2.5x
- Win: 2x
- Push (tie): 1x
- Loss: 0x

**House Edge:** ~0.5% (with basic strategy)

### Slots
**How to Play:**
- Click spin
- Match symbols across reels
- More matches = bigger payout

**Symbols:**
- Cherry: 10x
- Lemon/Orange/Plum: 15x
- Bell: 20x
- Bar: 25x
- Seven: 50x
- Gold: 100x

**House Edge:** 8%

### Bingo
**How to Play:**
- Mark numbers as they're called
- Complete patterns (row, column, diagonal, full card)
- First to complete wins

**Patterns:**
- Row: 5x
- Column: 5x
- Diagonal: 10x
- Full Card (Bingo): 50x

## Sports Betting

### Moneyline
**Predict the winner:**
- Home: Team A wins
- Draw: Match ends in draw
- Away: Team B wins

**Odds:** Vary by matchup

### Spread
**Predict winner with point margin:**
- Home -2.5: Home team wins by more than 2.5 points
- Away +2.5: Away team loses by less than 2.5 points

### Totals (Over/Under)
**Predict total points/goals:**
- Over 45.5: Total points > 45.5
- Under 45.5: Total points < 45.5

### Prop Bets
**Specific outcomes:**
- First goalscorer
- Total corners
- Yellow/Red cards
- Player statistics

## Fairness Verification

### Provably Fair System
1. Place bet
2. Receive server seed hash
3. Play game
4. After result, receive server seed
5. Verify: Hash(server seed) matches pre-game hash
6. Verify: Result matches calculated random value

### How to Verify
- Go to bet history
- Click "Verify"
- Enter server seed
- System confirms fairness
